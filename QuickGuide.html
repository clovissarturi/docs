TennisCount works 100% offline. No internet connection required. **Q: Do I need an iPhone?** A: No. TennisCount runs entirely on your Apple Watch. **Q: Does it collect my data?** A: No. We don't collect any data. Everything stays on your watch. Read our [Privacy Policy](link-to-privacy-policy). **Q: Which Apple Watch models are supported?** A: Apple Watch Series 4 and later, running watchOS 9.0 or later. ### Scoring & Rules **Q: How does tennis scoring work?** A: Points go: 0 (love), 15, 30, 40, Game. At 40-40 (deuce), a player must win by 2 points (advantage system). **Q: What is tiebreak?** A: When a set reaches 6-6, a tiebreak is played. First to 7 (or 10/12) points with a 2-point lead wins. **Q: What is simplified mode?** A: In simplified mode, you score games directly without tracking individual points (love, 15, 30, 40). Perfect for casual matches. **Q: Can I change the number of sets?** A: Yes! Configure 1, 3, or 5 sets in Settings before starting a match. **Q: How does the server indicator work?** A: The tennis ball icon shows who's serving. It automatically rotates after each game. At the start, tap an icon to choose the first server. **Q: How do I undo a mistake?** A: Swipe left on any player's zone to undo the last point. You can undo multiple points in a row. ### Features **Q: What is voice score announcement?** A: Enable in Settings to hear the score announced after each point. Works in 11 languages! **Q: How do I share match results?** A: After a match ends, tap the Share button on the statistics screen. You can send results via Messages, Mail, or social media. **Q: Can I see past matches?** A: Yes! Tap the History icon on the home screen to view all completed matches. **Q: Can I delete matches from history?** A: Yes. Swipe left on any match in History to delete it, or use "Clear All" to delete everything. **Q: What languages are supported?** A: English, Portuguese, Spanish, French, Italian, German, Russian, Chinese, Hindi, Bengali, and Japanese. ### Troubleshooting **Q: Double Tap isn't working. Why?** A: 1. Make sure you have Apple Watch Series 9 or Ultra 2 2. Wake the watch (Double Tap doesn't work in Always On mode) 3. Enable Double Tap in Settings 4. Ensure you're performing the gesture correctly (pinch index and thumb twice quickly) **Q: Voice announcements aren't working. Why?** A: 1. Check that Voice Score is enabled in Settings 2. Ensure your watch volume is up 3. Make sure Do Not Disturb is off 4. Try restarting the app **Q: The app is slow or laggy. What can I do?** A: 1. Force quit the app (press and hold side button, then press Digital Crown) 2. Restart your Apple Watch 3. Ensure you're running the latest watchOS version 4. If problem persists, uninstall and reinstall the app **Q: Can I use this for doubles matches?** A: Currently, TennisCount is designed for singles. Doubles support may come in a future update. **Q: Can I track advanced statistics (aces, faults, etc.)?** A: Not yet, but we're considering this for future versions. Let us know if you'd like this feature! **Q: Why can't I edit scores after the match ends?** A: To maintain match integrity, scores are final once a match ends. Use Undo during the match to fix mistakes. --- ## üêõ Report a Bug Found a bug?
